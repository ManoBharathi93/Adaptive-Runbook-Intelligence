========================================
HR POLICY & PROCEDURES MANUAL
========================================

## LEAVE POLICY
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year for full‑time employees; pro‑rated for part‑time.
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year; medical certificate required for absences > 3 consecutive days.
- Emergency Leave: up to 5 days for immediate‑family emergencies; manager approval required within 24 hours.
- Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid (birth parent), 4 weeks paid (non‑birth parent).
- Unpaid Leave: may be granted for up to 30 days with VP approval.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance (except emergencies).
- Leave balance is visible in the HR self‑service portal.

## PAYROLL PROCEDURES
- Pay cycle: bi‑weekly (every other Friday).
- Overtime is calculated at 1.5× for hours exceeding 40/week (non‑exempt employees).
- Payroll discrepancies must be reported within 30 days of the affected pay date.
- Corrections are processed in the next pay cycle after approval.
- Direct deposit changes take one pay cycle to become effective.
- Bonus payouts follow the quarterly performance review schedule.

## ONBOARDING PROCESS
- Offer letter and background check must be completed before Day 1.
- Day 1 checklist: badge provisioning, system account creation, workstation setup, orientation session.
- IT accounts (email, VPN, SSO) should be provisioned 2 business days before start date.
- Mandatory training modules must be completed within the first 14 days:
  * Security Awareness
  * Code of Conduct
  * Data Privacy (GDPR / CCPA)
  * Anti‑Harassment

## BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
- Open enrollment occurs annually in November.
- New hires have a 30‑day enrollment window starting from their hire date.
- Plans available: Medical (PPO / HMO), Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, 401(k).
- Changes outside open enrollment require a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, etc.).
- Benefits questions should be directed to benefits@company.com or the HR portal.

## WORKPLACE CONDUCT
- All employees must adhere to the Code of Conduct policy.
- Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and subject to disciplinary action.
- Complaints can be filed through the Ethics Hotline (anonymous) or directly with HR.
- Investigation SLA: acknowledgment within 2 business days, resolution within 30 days.
- Retaliation against complainants is a terminable offense.

## PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
- Reviews occur quarterly with a formal annual review in Q4.
- Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs) last 60 days with bi‑weekly check‑ins.
- Promotion eligibility requires at least 12 months in current role with "Exceeds Expectations" rating.

## OFFBOARDING
- Resignation notice: 2 weeks minimum (4 weeks for Director+).
- Exit interview conducted by HR on the last working day.
- IT access revocation within 1 hour of the employee's last day.
- Final paycheck includes accrued leave balance (where legally required).

## ESCALATION FOR HR TICKETS
- Standard HR tickets (leave, payroll questions): resolved by HR Operations within 2 business days.
- Sensitive matters (complaints, investigations): routed to Employee Relations, handled confidentially.
- Executive‑level matters: escalated to CHRO directly.
