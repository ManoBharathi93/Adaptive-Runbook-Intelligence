========================================
IT SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE
========================================

## PASSWORD RESET POLICY
- Users may request up to 3 password resets per month via self‑service.
- After the third reset the account is flagged for security review.
- Temporary passwords expire after 24 hours and must be changed on first login.
- Service account password resets require approval from the owning team lead.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, digit, and special character.
- Password history: last 10 passwords cannot be reused.
- Multi‑factor authentication (MFA) must be re‑enrolled after a password reset.

## ACCOUNT UNLOCK PROCEDURE
- Accounts lock after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.
- Tier‑1 support can unlock standard user accounts immediately.
- Admin and service accounts require Tier‑2 approval before unlock.
- If the account was locked due to suspected brute force, escalate to Security Operations.
- Unlock actions are logged in the audit trail for 90 days.

## VPN ACCESS MANAGEMENT
- VPN access is granted based on role and manager approval.
- Contractors receive time‑limited VPN tokens (max 90 days, renewable).
- Full‑tunnel VPN is required for accessing production environments.
- Split‑tunnel VPN is allowed for general corporate access.
- VPN connection issues should first be triaged with: ping test → DNS check → client reinstall.
- Persistent VPN disconnections may indicate network‑level policy conflicts; escalate to Network Engineering.

## SOFTWARE INSTALLATION REQUESTS
- Standard software catalog items can be auto‑approved for most roles.
- Non‑catalog software requires IT Security review (SLA: 3 business days).
- Admin/root privileges are not granted by default; exceptions need VP‑level approval.
- Development tools (IDEs, runtimes) are pre‑approved for Engineering and Data Science roles.

## EMAIL / MESSAGING ISSUES
- If a user reports no incoming email, check mailbox quota and transport rules first.
- Shared mailbox permission changes require the mailbox owner's approval.
- Email outages affecting multiple users should be escalated to the Exchange / M365 team immediately.
- Deleted emails can be recovered from the soft‑delete retention folder (14‑day window).

## ESCALATION MATRIX
- Tier 1 → Tier 2: unresolved after 30 minutes or outside Tier 1 scope.
- Tier 2 → Tier 3: requires infrastructure changes or vendor involvement.
- Critical Priority: respond within 15 minutes, resolve within 4 hours.
- High Priority: respond within 30 minutes, resolve within 8 hours.
- Medium Priority: respond within 2 hours, resolve within 24 hours.
- Low Priority: respond within 4 hours, resolve within 48 hours.

## GENERAL SECURITY GUIDELINES
- Never share credentials over email or chat.
- All remote sessions must use encrypted channels.
- Report suspected phishing to security@company.com immediately.
- USB storage devices are disabled on all corporate endpoints by default.
